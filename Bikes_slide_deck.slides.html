Ford GoBike: Bike rides data in San Francisco Bay Area: 2019¶

Investigation Overview:¶

In this investigation, I would like to know the number of bike rides in Bay area, in relation to months, day amd time of day, average biking time and its distribution among subscribers and customers.¶

Dataset Overview:¶

The dataset has 2506983 observations, with attributes like station names, ride timestamp as start time and end time, duration and types of users (customer/subscriber). Few obseravations were considered as outliers, and were removed during exploration.¶

Rides distribution according to:¶

1: Month of year and day of week¶

It can be seen that the highest number of rides is during July, followed by March and least are in December (most probably because of cold weather and shorter day light duration).¶

Average biking duration¶

When we just check average ride duration for the whole data, we see there are few outliers, and removing them gives a better ideas that the average biking duration is around 10 min, with highest being around 28 min. If histogram is checked, then its clear that it is right skewed, with most bikers duration falling in range of 5-10 min, and then decreasing with increasing time.¶

Bike rides duration according to users and months of year/ Days of week¶

What is observe is that the average biking duration is high in case of costomers irrespective on any month of the year. And also, the duration is between 15 to 18 minutes, with a significant drop for month of December. For subscribers, the duration is pretty contant throug out the year, ranging from 9.993 to 11.225, with minimum being in February.¶
